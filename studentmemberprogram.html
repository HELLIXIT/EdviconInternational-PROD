Student Member Path and Requirements

Student Members are required to complete the requirements mentioned to advanced to the next milestone. Each milestone has unique benefits additionally to previous milestone.

1

Student Member Milestone

* Being a School/College/University student
2

Omega Student Member Milestone

* 5 Referrals for Edvicon Int. registration
* Edvicon Int. Google Business page review
* Edvicon Int. Facebook page review
3

Beta Student Member Milestone

* 5 Additional referrals for Edvicon Int. registration
* Contributed to Premium Resource Directory
4

Alpha Student Member Milestone

* 5 Additional referrals for Edvicon Int. registration
* An article published on Medium/ LinkedIn about Edvicon Int. OR mentioning Edvicon Int. with contact information. 
5

Student Ambassador Milestone

* 5 Additional referrals for Edvicon Int. registration

Student Members should report completed activities using below form. Login with your issued Microsoft 365 account to access the submission form.
