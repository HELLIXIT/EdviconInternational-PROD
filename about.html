About Us

Learning with Love and Laughter

The Edvicon International was established on the 28th of March, 2020 with the intention of making decent industry-oriented professionals. For the great cause, Edvicon International offers many benefits for the members of the organization.

Edvicon International is a volunteer-driven, non-profit organization registered under the Digital Infrastructure & Information Technology Division, Ministry of Defense, Sri Lanka.

Member Benefits
  • Microsoft Office 365
  • Microsoft Products for Education
  • G Suite for Education
  • Edvicon Email Accounts with Benefits
  • Edvicon Community Membership

VISION

Forge strong, positive connections with students so they can achieve independence, build confidence, and gain academic knowledge

MISSION

Develop well-rounded and thoughtful students prepared to cope with a changing post-modern and globalized world

OBJECTIVES

The Best for You

  • Create personals with highest professional qualifications.
  • Share Educational resources to enhance the education experience.
  • Make use of newest and the latest technologies.
  • Promote the open education in public.
  • Encourage and assist students in their studies.
  • Help members to become more productive.
  • Opens up a host of opportunities where technology and education ensure that opportunities are not bound by geography.
  • Empower members to turn weaknesses into strengths.
  • Enhance Innovation and Creativity of members.
  • Socializing and Networking of members.
  • Personal Development of Skills.
  • Career Preparation of members.
